Football Fashion

My project idea is based around using augmented reality to try on Football Shirts from different clubs or teams. This is just the basic idea with the ability to develop further into other sports or even the fashion industry,

Football Fashion is based around being able to try out ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿different sports or football shirts without having to go to the stores themselves. This would be really useful in places like NZ where you dont have access to try on most of the different types of football shirts.
